Biography

    I am currently a Research Scientist at NEC Laboratories America located in San Jose, CA, USA. I have been interested in Distributed Optimization, Federated Learning, Graph and Game Theory, and Blockchain. I have industrial and developement expertise in Networks and Distributed Systems, Cloud and IoT Computing, and Open-Standard Protocols Integration and has developed a variety of infrastructure platforms and applications for Transportation Management, Smart Energy, Network Traffic Engineering, Fault Tolerance, Anomaly Detection. among others.
    Before joining NEC Labs, he was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Fujitsu Laboratories of America in Sunnyvale.
    I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at UC Irvine in 2014 where I was involved in a project on Smart Energy Control Model with Professors Lubomir Bic, Michael Dillencourt, and Jack Brouwer. I also collaborated with Smart Grid Research Group at California Institute of Technology. Besides this project, I have collaborated with Prof. Nalini Venkatasubramanian for Network Fault Tolerance Project, and with Prof. Eli Bozorgzadeh for Energy-Havesting Wireless Sensor Network Project.
