I am currently a Research Scientist at NEC Laboratories America located in San Jose, CA, USA. At NEC Labs, I am currently leading a project on decentralized Blockchain and Federated Learning framework for various AI applications for public infrastructure, transportation, and energy management. My research interests these days are Distributed AI and Blockchain. I have industrial and developement expertise in Networks and Distributed Systems, Cloud and IoT Computing, and Open-Standard Protocols Integration and has developed a variety of infrastructure platforms and applications. At Fujitsu labs, I was intensively engaged in prototyping an automated demand response system with data analytics and visualization features, which also supports various open-standard communications protocols. In my PhD at UCI, I digged into distributed network systems, graph theory, and a new smart energy control framework. Fortunately, two papers received the Best Paper Award.
