Brief Biography

    Kiyoshi completed his Ph.D. Degree in June 2014 and is now at NEC Laboratories America located in Cupertino working as a Research Scientist in the Energy Management Department.
    Before joining NEC Labs, he was a Postdoctoral Researcher in Smart Energy Group at Fujitsu Laboratories of America in Sunnyvale.
    Kiyoshi Nakayama was in the Ph.D. Program in Computer Science at University of California, Irvine, where he initiated a project on Smart Energy Control Model with Professors Lubomir Bic, Michael Dillencourt, and Jack Brouwer. He also collaborated with Smart Grid Research Group at California Institute of Technology. Besides the Dissertation Project, Kiyoshi has conducted Fault Tolerance Project with Prof. Nalini Venkatasubramanian, Energy-Havesting Wireless Sensor Network (EHWSN) Project with Prof. Eli Bozorgzadeh, and Network Flow Optimization Project.
